# Irradiance

Irradiance is the light falling on *unit area* of sensor per second.
$$
I \propto N^2
$$
where $N$ is the relative [[aperture]].
$$
I \propto 1 / f^2
$$
where $f$ is the [[focalLength|focal length]].
