# Exposure

Exposure is the total amount of light received by the camera sensor. This is controlled by [[aperture]], [[shutterSpeed|shutter speed]], and [[ISO]].

This is expressed by 
$$
H = E \times t
$$
where $E$ is the [[irradiance]] and $t$ is time.
